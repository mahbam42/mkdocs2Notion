# Multi-level Lists

## Bulleted

- Warehouse intake
  - Verify packing slip against PO
    - Flag shortages with a *stock adjustment* note
    - Schedule follow-up delivery when needed
  - Stage items on the correct rack
    - Group by destination to keep counts clear
      - Confirm serial numbers before sealing crates

## Ordered

1. Prepare export bundle
   1. Run the final inventory check
   2. Capture screenshots for audit evidence
      1. Highlight discrepancies with **bold callouts**
2. Publish to the review channel
   - Mention the on-call approver
   - Attach the reconciled CSV
3. Archive artifacts for the monthly report
